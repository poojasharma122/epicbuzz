Best Pet-Friendly Resorts in Canada That Don’t Treat Dogs Like Luggage

You don’t leave the trail dog behind. These stays not only allow pets — they’re built for them. Think long forest walks, dog-friendly trails, and zero side-eye at check-in.

Moraine Lake Lodge – Alberta

Pet-friendly cabins with views of one of the most iconic alpine lakes in Canada. You’ll need a reservation and a leash — but dogs are welcome, and the scenery is ridiculous.

Black Rock Oceanfront Resort – British Columbia

Located in Ucluelet, this wild West Coast retreat offers oceanfront trails and pet-approved rooms with easy access to the beach.

Fernie Fox Hotel – British Columbia

Clean, cozy, and totally geared toward travellers with dogs. Trailheads nearby. Waste stations outside. No extra charge for furry friends.

Hotel Le Germain Charlevoix – Quebec

Stylish, central, and super dog-friendly. Walkable trails, on-site bowls, and a modern vibe that doesn’t skimp on warmth.

Deerhurst Resort – Ontario

Set on a sprawling lakefront property, Deerhurst has specific pet-friendly rooms, dog swimming zones, and forest paths for pre-dinner fetch.
